## 🛠️ Verification & Transparency
1.  **Direct Transfer:** ACSP uses SSL Commerz for secure, direct routing of funds to NGO bank accounts.
2.  **Audit Trail:** Every transaction is recorded with a unique Reference ID (REF) linked to the payment gateway's Validation ID (valId).
3.  **NGO Access:** Partner NGOs can view their specific donation history via their dedicated NGO Admin Dashboard.
4.  **Tax Receipts:** Automated donation receipts are sent to registered donors for their contributions.
