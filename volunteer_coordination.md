## 📋 Coordination Guidelines
1.  **Application:** All volunteers must complete the online application and a brief background check.
2.  **Training:** NGO-specific training (e.g., animal handling, medical protocols) is mandatory before first shift.
3.  **Logging Hours:** Volunteers should log their hours via the NGO Admin dashboard to receive certificates.
4.  **Recognition:** Top contributors are highlighted on the ACSP "Heroes" wall monthly.
