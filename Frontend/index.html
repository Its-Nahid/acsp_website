Our Vision & Mission

ACSP was born from a vision to centralize and professionalize animal welfare efforts in Bangladesh. As a student-led initiative, we are building a digital ecosystem that empowers rescuers, supports NGOs, and connects animal lovers to create a unified force for compassion.

Common Questions

How can I report an injured animal?

expand_more
You can use the 'Report an Animal' button on the homepage to upload a photo and location. Our system will immediately dispatch the information to the nearest partner NGO.

Is the adoption process free?

expand_more
ACSP is a non-profit platform, but some NGOs may have a small processing fee to cover vaccination and administrative costs for the animal.

Where does my donation go?

expand_more
100% of your donation goes directly to the selected NGO or animal case. ACSP does not take any commission; we are funded by separate grants and sponsors.

How do I become a partner NGO?

expand_more
Organizations can apply through the 'Register' page by selecting the 'NGO Admin' role. We require verification of your NGO's registration status in Bangladesh.

Can I volunteer as a veterinarian?

expand_more
Yes! Licensed veterinarians can register with a 'Veterinarian' role to help with consultations and emergency triage through our AI-assisted portal.
